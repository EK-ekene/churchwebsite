About us

Who we are

Christ Apostolic Church Bubiashie Central is a thriving Pentecostal congregation located in the heart of Bubiashie. With a rich history dating back to 1990's, our church is committed to fostering a strong relationship with God, nurturing spiritual growth, and building a close-knit church family. Our vibrant worship services, inspiring sermons, and engaging programs cater to people of all ages, backgrounds, and walks of life. At CAC Bubiashie Central, you'll find a welcoming environment where individuals and families can discover and deepen their faith journey together. As a church, we are dedicated to making a positive impact on our local community through various outreach initiatives, charity work, and support programs. Join us every Sunday at 8:00 AM for an uplifting worship experience and connect with like-minded believers during our midweek service on Wednesdays at 6:00 PM. We believe that every person has a unique role to play in God's plan, and we invite you to become a part of our mission to spread love, hope, and the message of Christ's redemption. Come and experience the joy of worship and fellowship at Christ Apostolic Church Bubiashie Central. We look forward to meeting you and growing in faith together!


Our Mission

CAC (International) as part of the body of christ , exist to bring salvation in Christ to all persons in the world by preaching and teaching the gospel of Jesus Christ as in the Holy Scriptures , the final authority in all matters of faith and conduct , discipline believers towards Christian maturity and worshipping the True God-The Father, Son and Holy Spirit. Its objectives are threefold namely: spiritual which cover doctrinal issues and social objectives which comprises the activities that contribute to the social and economic development of its members and other needy groups or the communities


Our Vision

Knowing Christ and making Christ-like disciples of all nations, tribes and peoples and constituting them into bible fellowships for worship, edification and further evangelization
